Important Points
lEvery chromosome can be identified as having come from either Mom or Pop
lHumans have 23 different chromosomes, but are paired giving us a total of 46 – a set of 23 from mom and a set of 23 from dad. 
lEvery chromosome can be identified by the specific genes they contain
lFor example, in humans, chromosome 1 has a gene that helps determine brain size – and chromosome 3 has a gene that helps determine blood type
