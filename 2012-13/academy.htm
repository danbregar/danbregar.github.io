Raider Academy

 

Raider Academy is a class for incoming 9th graders. Our curriculum will center around service learning opportunities and associated academic learning (writing, math, and reading/research). We will focus on organizational skills, decision-making, time-management, and other skills that are critical to success in and beyond high school.
