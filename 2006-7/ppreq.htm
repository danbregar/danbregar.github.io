Seminar PowerPoint Presentation Requirements

Biology

 

  • Your presentation should consist of 10-15 slides, each taking 1-2 minutes to explain, for a total presentation length of about 20 minutes.

 

  • You should have roughly an equal number of slides for each viewpoint of your topic you discuss.

 

  • All your slides should contain information about their sources (use small fonts; it’s OK to put this information at the bottom of your slide)

 

  • Your presentation should focus on being informative – what is the issue (use definitions if necessary), what are the viewpoints, and what is the science behind the controversy?

 

  • The last one or two slides should have:

1.   A quick discussion of what types of legislation, litigation, or controversies are on the horizon for your topic (for example, in the evolution / intelligent design controversy, a number of states have legislation pending that would require schools to teach intelligent design)

2.   Six to eight discussion questions for the class – these should be information and fact-based, NOT debate topics
