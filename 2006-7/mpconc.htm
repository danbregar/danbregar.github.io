Mushroom Project Results and Conclusion

 

To answer the main question of your study, you will need to compare the production of mushrooms on your substrate to the production of mushrooms on the control substrate (straw).  Do this on a PER BAG basis (in other words, divide your data by the number of substrate bags you used).

 

The measurements you should take today are:

1.   How many mushrooms are on each bag (average)?

2.   What is the volume of mushroom material on each bag (average)?  To estimate volume, measure the height and radius of each clump of mushrooms, then use the formula V = pi * r * r * h.

 

This data should show up on your excel spreadsheet.  A graph of this data should be used as one of your results slides (you can use two graphs if you’d like).  Your conclusion slide should discuss the answer to the question:  “Which substrate is more efficient at producing mushrooms:  straw or _____?”.
