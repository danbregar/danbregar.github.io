Mushroom Project Goals – 3/7 and 3/8/07

Biology and Field Biology

 

1.   Complete as much of your PowerPoint presentation as you can – you can’t do the results and conclusion section until your bags produce mushrooms, but you should complete the rest of the slides.  There will be no more class time for working on this part of your presentation.

 

2.   Measure pH and temperature of your mushroom bags (CO2 is optional)

 

3.   Prepare an Excel spreadsheet with ALL of your pH, temperature, and CO2 data.  Create graphs from this data as well.  This information should be used for the “data” section of your presentation.

 

4.   Check your mushroom bags – if primordia are present, punch small holes in the bag with a sterilized pair of dissecting scissors.

 

5.   Place your mushroom bag in the small display room – make sure all the holes are unobstructed, and spray your mushroom bag with water to hydrate and humidify.
