Objective:

The goal of my Rube Goldberg Machine is to take a piece of paper and fold it into a paper airplane.  The first step in my Rube Goldberg Machine was that I pressed on a lever.  The lever caused a string to be released, which caused a pulley to turn because there was a weight on the other end.  When the pulley turned, a stick that was attached to it pushed on a piece of paper that was suspended on two pieces of wood.  The weight from the pulley fell on another lever, which hit a marble that was balanced at the top of a ramp.  When the marble rolled down the ramp, it hit another piece of wood that was supposed to fold the piece of paper again (but it didn't work).  That piece of wood also hit another lever, which hit another marble that was balanced on a ramp.  When that marble rolled down the ramp, it hit another piece of wood that was supposed to make the third fold in the piece of paper (it didn't work either).
