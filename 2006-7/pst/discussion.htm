Discussion:

The motion of the lever is caused by the weight pushing down on it.  The table and gravity are pushing in opposite directions, but with the same strength - so they balance each other out.  The force of the weight is not balanced - so the lever accelerates in the direction of the force of the weight.

The motion of the marble is caused by gravity, friction, and the ramp.  Gravity is pulling straight down on the marble.  The surface of the ramp is pushing the marble at a 90 degree angle to the surface of the ramp.  Friction is pushing the marble up the ramp (opposite the direction of motion).  When these forces are combined, it causes an overall force down the ramp, so the marble accelerates down the ramp.
