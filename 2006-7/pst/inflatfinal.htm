Final Inflatable Structures

Physical Science and Technology

 

Now that you have an accurate scale layout drawing, you’re ready to transfer those measurements to the actual sheet of plastic you’ll use to construct your structure.  Keep in mind that all your scale measurements will be multiplied by ten in order to figure out the actual measurement on the plastic.  You MAY need to use a different multiplier (2, 3, or 5 are good numbers to pick) if your plastic isn’t big enough for your drawings.

 

Once you have carefully measured the lines on the plastic, you can cut out your shapes and tape them together to form the final structure.  To add the fan, cut a hole along the side of your structure and tape the fan in securely (you want your structure to be as air-tight as possible).
