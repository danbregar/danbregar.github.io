Overview
lMeiosis is a type of cell division in sexually reproducing organisms that results in cells with half the chromosome number of the original cell.
lHappens in 2 phases: Meiosis 1, and Meiosis 2.
lPhase 1 increase the amount of genetic variation & reduces chromosome #
lPhase 2 separates sister chromatids
lNew cells created by meiotic division are gametes (egg and sperm cells in animals).
lMutations that occur in cells that perform meiosis are heritable (can be passed on from parent to offspring).
