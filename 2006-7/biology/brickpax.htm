Your group of five - six needs to prepare two identical bricks!

-  Work in two groups of three (or one of two and one of three)

-  Find two bricks of the same size, shape, and material

-  Tie 25 or 30 leaves to each brick – distribute them in exactly the same way (use twine)

-  Make sure each brick has a “leash” of 10 – 15 feet

-  Label your leash – write your period and your group’s initials on a piece of masking tape and wrap it around your leash
