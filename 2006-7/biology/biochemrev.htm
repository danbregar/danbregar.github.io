Biochemistry Quiz Review

Biology

 

In preparation for the quiz, you should understand how to answer the following questions:

 

1.   What are the unique properties of water?

2.   What is a polar molecule?

3.   What is the difference between an ionic and a covalent bond?

4.   What are the names and functions of the four major categories of biochemical substances?

5.   What types of substances will dissolve in water?  What types of substances won’t dissolve in water?

6.   What are the three categories of chemical reactions we discussed in class?  What is one example of each type?

7.   What is one reason chemical reactions are so important to living organisms?  (Hint – what was the “missing” product in the chemical reactions we looked at in class?)

8.   How can visible light indicate how much energy is being given off by a chemical reaction?

9.   What are the unique properties of phospholipids?

10. What are phospholipid bi-layers and why are they so important to living organisms?

 

For the quiz, you may use one sheet of notes (front and back) - make sure you have all the information we discussed in class and in readings!
