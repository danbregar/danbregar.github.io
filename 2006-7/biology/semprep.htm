Seminar Preparation

Biology

 

Before you give your seminar presentation, you and your partner should have done the following:

 

1.   Make sure that your PowerPoint presentation is complete and meets all the requirements specified

 

2.   Each of you should have a copy of your PowerPoint presentation for rehearsal

 

3.   Make an outline or notecards, if necessary, so you know what you’re going to say

*    I recommend that rather than using an outline or notecards, you use your PowerPoint presentation as your cue (it works just like a notecard).  In order to do this well, you will have to have a very strong understanding of your topic – you don’t necessarily have to memorize facts and figures, but you should have a good overall understanding of what you will be discussing.  You should make sure that there are no terms about which you are unclear.

 

4.   Check the schedule (posted online) to make sure you know when your presentation is!
