1.   Define the following terms:

a.   Evolution

b.   Allele Frequency

c.   Population

d.   Natural Selection

e.   Differential Reproduction

f.    Speciation

g.   Reproductive Isolation

h.   Variation

i.    Mutation

 

2.   In the “Predator” game, describe a hunting strategy that would result in NO EVOLUTION of the dots over time.  Assume that the colors of the dots are determined genetically (i.e., each dot color is represented by a different allele of the “color” gene).

 

3.   For the Stickworms, describe a likely scenario under which reproductive isolation could occur.  Make sure you explain WHY the separate groups would not interbreed.

 

4.   Describe a situation – real or imagined – in which natural selection is occurring, but speciation is not.

 

5.   Describe a situation – real or imagined – in which reproductive isolation and natural selection leads to speciation.  What criteria could you use to determine whether or not different populations are different species?
