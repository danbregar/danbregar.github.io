Your Job
1.Use string, paper, paper clips, and markers to model the starting and ending points of meiosis
2.Start by showing a diploid cell with four chromosomes from Mom and four chromosomes from Pop – these chromosomes should already have been replicated, so they will appear as sister chromatids (make all attachments loosely!)
3.Each chromosome should be clearly identifiable by number (1, 2, 3, or 4) and parental source (Mom or Pop)
4.Show an instructor and get checked off before you continue!
5.Now, rearrange the genetic material to create four new haploid cells
6.Each cell should have one full set of chromosomes
7.Show an instructor again to make sure you’ve got it right!
