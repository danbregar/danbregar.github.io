Seminar Summary Sheet

Biology

 

For this assignment, you should create a one page handout that contains the following information:

 

1.   A one paragraph introduction to your seminar topic

2.   An outline of the information in your seminar – this can be bulleted, but unlike your PowerPoint presentation, you should include ALL the information you plan to say.  (In your PowerPoint, your bullets are short because what you say will expand on the ideas.  On this summary sheet, all the information needs to be there for people to read.)  You should include citations in this section.

3.   Your discussion questions

 

This handout will be used by students who are absent during your seminar presentation, so it should contain all of the information you plan to include in your seminar.
