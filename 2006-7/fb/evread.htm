Evolution Reading Assignment

Field Biology

 

Read the article on my website entitled “Intelligent Design and the Story of Evolution”.

 

Based on the reading, answer the following questions:

1.   According to the author, science is not really the process of finding out the “Truth” to how things REALLY work.  Explain the author’s argument regarding the distinction between “Truth” and science.

2.   If science is not the “Truth”, does that mean that all science (and particularly evolutionary biology) is Wrong?

3.   How would the author describe the criteria that an idea or theory must meet in order to be called a “scientific” theory?  (Hint – what does the author say a good “scientific story” accomplishes?)

4.   According to the author, does evolution and evolutionary theory mean that Intelligent Design is False?

5.   Since the author admits that science can and will change (based on observed evidence), does this invalidate science as a way of understanding the world?  In other words, does it make much sense to rely on any particular viewpoint if it’s likely to change at some point in the future?

 

Write the answers on a sheet of notebook paper and turn them in to the turkey bin.
