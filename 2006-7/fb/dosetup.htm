Instructions for Setting up Dissolved Oxygen and pH Probes

 

To Set Up Dissolved Oxygen Probe:

1.                  Unscrew probe cap

2.                  Using the eyedropper, fill the cap with electrode filling solution

3.                  Screw the cap back onto the probe

4.                  Rinse with distilled water

5.                  Plug the probe into the computer, start LoggerPro, and load the Dissolved Oxygen file from the Water Quality folder

6.                  Let the probe warm up for 15 – 30 minutes

 

To Store the Dissolved Oxygen Probe:

1.                  Remove the probe cap and gently dump out the electrode filling solution

2.                  Rinse the probe tip and cap with distilled water

3.                  Blot the probe tip and cap dry with a Kimwipe

4.                  Loosely screw the cap back onto the probe and wrap the cables

5.                  Place all the equipment back in the box

 

To Set Up pH Probe:

1.         Unscrew O-ring cap

2.         Remove pH probe

3.         Remove O-ring cap from probe

4.         Rinse with distilled water

 

To Store pH Probe:

1.         Rinse with distilled water

2.         Slide O-ring cap back onto probe

3.         Screw bottle back onto O-ring cap
