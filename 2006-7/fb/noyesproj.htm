Noyes Project Parameters

Field Biology

 

 

1.                  Everyone in the class is responsible for data collection, data entry, and data analysis.

2.                  You will work in partners.

3.                  We are going to have one overarching goal for the whole class.

4.                  We’ll have 5 90/95 minute blocks to collect data in the field.  (Our data is “snapshot” data – there’s no time to wait for changes.)

5.                  Safety is the most important consideration.

6.                  Our data needs to be 100% guaranteed to be collectable – no mammals or amphibians.

7.                  We need to know everything pertinent to our study by the end of the period on Friday, May 18.

a.       What will our data collection protocol be?

b.      We need data collection forms

c.       We need to know any special handling procedures for all of the factors we’re studying

d.      We need a detailed materials list

e.       Additional info?

8.                  You will need every day to have:

a.       Work clothes / field garb

b.      Closed-toe shoes or boots

c.       Work gloves

d.      Clippers

e.       Rain gear

9.                  Individually, you will produce:

a.       Written report

b.      Poster / PowerPoint presentation (extra credit may be given if you do a presentation for another science class)
