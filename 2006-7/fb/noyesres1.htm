Research Questions for Noyes Project

Field Biology (1st Period)

 

Question:  What is the relationship between the presence / absence of poison oak and the species of surrounding plants?

 

Material Needs:

  • Dichotomous key to identify surrounding plant species
  • Equipment for measuring plots and quadrants (1m x 1m)
  • Clippers
  • Data Sheets

 

General Research Questions:

  • What makes Poison Oak poisonous?

 

  • What plant species are (likely to be) at the Noyes property?  (Look at plant lists for McDonald Forest and at the Willamette Gardens website.)

 

  • How competitive is Poison Oak?

 

  • What type of habitat does Poison Oak prefer?

 

Data Sheet:

  • Presence / absence of Poison Oak (estimate of abundance low – high)
  • Date / Time
  • Team
  • Plot #
  • Quadrant ID
  • Species Checklist
