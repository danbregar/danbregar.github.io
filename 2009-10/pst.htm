PST

 

 

Physical Science and Technology is a class that is designed to allow students to learn to use technological tools, design and build principles, and physical science concepts.  Students will use computers, electronic instruments, wood and metal working tools, recording equipment, and chemical tests to examine the physical and chemical properties of the world around them.

 

This class is team taught with Adam Kirsch.
