Assignment #16 – Community Web Site Draft

Web Design

 

This week, you and your group will add text, graphics, buttons, and links to your community web site pages.

 

Your goal is for each of you to have completed two pages of the overall website – so if there are two people in your group, your web site needs four pages; if there are three people in your group, your web site needs six pages.

 

All the pages should match the characteristics of your template page – they should have the same layout, font, and color scheme; text and graphics should be laid out in a consistent and attractive way; and hover buttons should be identical.

 

You should save all these pages on the same H: drive that you stored the template page.  One of you should be working on an “index.htm” page – this is the page that I will first load to check the rest of your work.

 

At the bottom of each page, please type the name of the person who designed that page and put the date of the latest revision (for example, “Last update on 5/15/06 by Dan Bregar”).  I will be grading your pages separately, so it is important that you put your name on the page for full credit.
