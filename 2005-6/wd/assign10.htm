Assignment #10 - Details for Teacher Web Site

Web Design

 

For this assignment, each person in your teacher web site group needs to do three things:

 

1.   Using existing resources (the yearbook, the on-line course catalog, an existing teacher web page or blackboard site, etc.) compile as much information as possible related to the specific web page you are designing for the teacher you are working with.  This information should be hand-written as you go, since you will not always necessarily be right next to a computer as you gather this information.

 

2.   Schedule a meeting time with the teacher you are working with when you can spend between 10 and 15 minutes with your entire group discussing the specifics of the web page you are designing.  This meeting should take place after spring break.

 

3.   Make a list of 5 – 10 specific questions you will ask your teacher during your meeting time.  These questions should be directed towards generating all of the information you need to complete your web page for this teacher.  Remember, the maximum time you have to work with the teacher is 15 minutes per group – so your questions should be short and specific.
