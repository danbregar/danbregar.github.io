Assignment #11 – Teacher Web Site Revisions

Web Design

 

For this assignment, you will be making initial revisions of your teacher web site.

 

This should be done as follows:

 

1.   Schedule a time with the teacher whose web page you are making – this should be a time when you, your group members, and the teacher can sit down for 10 minutes or so, in front of a computer.  At that time, you should show the teacher your web site and ask for comments and suggestions.  This time should be scheduled sometime BEFORE Thursday, May 4 (so you will be able to make these changes on Thursday, May 4 and Friday, May 5).

 

2.   Make any changes you think are necessary that were suggested by the other students in the class during the evaluations on Friday, April 21.

 

3.   Make any changes suggested by me.  Please note that the scores that I have given you are progress grades only – these are NOT going to be entered into the gradebook yet.

 

4.   Together with your group, look through your entire teacher website and discuss any changes you feel might be necessary.  Make sure that all the pages are consistent, that the site is easily navigable, and that the color scheme and font choices are clean and easy to look at.

 

I will give you another progress grade based on the work you have completed at the end of this week (Friday, April 28).

 

The final day for you to work on your teacher web site will be Friday, May 5.  I will give you a final score (that will be entered into the gradebook) based on the work you’ve done up to that point.  However, there will be additional assignments (not related to the teacher web site) between now and then – so you should not count on having 100% of the class time between now and May 5 available to work on your teacher web site.
