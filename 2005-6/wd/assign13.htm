Assignment #13 – Community Web Site Templates

Web Design

 

The next step in creating your community web site is to create a template – one page that will serve as the basis for all the other pages in your web site.  This page does not have to have any actual content on it – but it should clearly indicate:

 

1.   What the color scheme of the site will be

2.   What font, font size, and font style will be used in various areas of the page

3.   Where text, pictures and graphics will be located

4.   Where headings, menus, and hyperlinks will be located

 

In summary, this template should make it clear what your page will look like – fonts, colors, and layouts.  This should be a layout that could work with any of the potential organizations you have discussed with your group.

 

At the end of the period, please turn in a sheet of paper with the names of all the students in your group that clearly indicates where your template page is located (include the name of the student whose H: drive it is on, folders it is in, and the name of the file itself).
