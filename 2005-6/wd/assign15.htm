Assignment #15 – Community Web Site Final Preview

Web Design

 

Today, you and your group will evaluate each other’s preview pages in order to determine the final characteristics of your web site.

 

Your goal is to create one page that will demonstrate all of the visual elements of your community web site.  This page should include:

 

1.   The layout, font, and color scheme of your web site

2.   Example locations of text and graphics

3.   Hover buttons as a site menu

4.   Inline frames to separate various parts of the page

5.   Tables within each section to organize graphics and text

 

You should save this page on one group member’s H: drive, as “H:\WD\Community\preview.htm”.  On a sheet of scrap paper, please write the names of all the members of your group and indicate whose H: drive will have the preview page.  Turn this paper in to the instructor at the end of the period.
