Web Design

Project #2 – Completed Personal Web Site / Peer Evaluation

 

For this assignment, you and your peers will evaluate each other’s personal web sites.  The final criteria for your personal sites are as follows:

 

A.  At least five different web pages (all of which share the same color scheme, layout, and fonts!)

B.   A navigation bar / menu bar (this can be down the left-hand side of the screen or anywhere else you choose) that takes the viewer between the pages you are designing

C.  Appropriate images and content (please note that you should not use text or graphics that might be offensive to others – and also please do not link to sites that use text or graphics that might be offensive to others!)

D.  At least two image maps located somewhere within your personal web site.

 

On Friday, March 17, during 4th period, you will evaluate four other student web sites from the Web Design class; specifically, you will be looking for the four criteria above.  Your web site will also be evaluated by other students during this time, so you should make sure that your site meets the listed criteria before the beginning of 4th period on March 17th.
