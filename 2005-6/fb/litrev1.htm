Literature Review #1 – Field Biology

 

On a crisp, clean, new sheet of paper, write the following:

 

Author:  Who wrote this scientific paper?

 

Title:  What is the title of this paper?

 

Source:  What is the URL of this scientific paper?  (the http:// thingy)

 

Purpose / Question:  What was she trying to find out – how did we summarize her overall goals for this project?

 

Hypothesis:  What did she expect to find?  (This is not something we looked at in all the classes; this information is available on page 2 of the scientific paper in the last paragraph of the introduction.)

 

Methods:  How did she go about answering her question – how did we summarize the things she actually went out and did?

 

Data / Results:  What pieces of factual information that the author found did we extract from this paper?

 

Discussion / Conclusions:  This is not something we looked at in class – by reading the section “Implications of this research” on page 11 of the paper, try to summarize two or three key findings that the author arrived at by interpreting her data.
