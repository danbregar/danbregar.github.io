Study Guide – Aquatic Macroinvertebrate Quiz

Field Biology

 

1.       What are the different functional feeding groups we looked at in class?  Describe WHAT and HOW organisms in each functional feeding group eat.

 

2.       What is the advantage of using functional feeding groups to classify aquatic organisms instead of other types of classification schemes (such as carnivore, predator, etc.)?

 

3.       Discuss some of the different life cycles of aquatic macroinvertebrates.  Be sure to describe both complete and incomplete metamorphosis.  (Hint – draw pictures!)

 

4.       Why are aquatic macroinvertebrates so useful for evaluating water pollution?  Why might you use aquatic macroinvertebrates for this as opposed to, for example, chemically testing the water?

 

5.       Aquatic macroinvertebrates are divided into regions and sections.  What is the difference between these divisions?

 

6.       Why might it be important to understand some basic terminology about macroinvertebrate anatomy?

 

7.       Make sure you can correctly describe the defining features of the following aquatic macroinvertebrate orders:  Ephemeroptera (Mayflies), Diptera (true flies), Trichoptera (Caddies flies), Plectopera (Stoneflies).
