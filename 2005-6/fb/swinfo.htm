Field Trip Information – Thursday, November 18

SalmonWatch

 

This field trip will give you the opportunity to study salmon and their natural environment.  We will be visiting Clemens Park, along the Alsea River, to observe spawning salmon, look at aquatic macroinvertebrates, study water quality, and evaluate the riparian environment.  We will also be taking some additional water quality information using our Vernier Probes for comparison purposes.

 

We will also be spending some time at the Alsea Hatchery to get an idea of what hatcheries are and how they operate.

 

Parents:  I will have my cell phone with me at all times on this trip.  Please call me at 541/990-1496 if you need to contact me at any time.
