Requirements:

1.   Must be created in PowerPoint (one presentation per group)

2.   Include all of the relevant information from your final report, including:

a.   Introduction (2 – 3 slides total for your question, your rationale, your hypothesis, and your site location)

b.   Methods (1 - 2 slides, should be in past tense)

c.   Results (1 - 2 slides total for your raw data and calculated numbers)

d.   Analysis (2 - 3 slides total for your statistical analysis, conclusions, generalizations, and implications of your study – should be in past tense where appropriate)

e.   Acknowledgements (1 slide) - thank anyone who provided you with help

3.   Should be around 4-5 minutes per person (I will give more details on Monday, January 24)
