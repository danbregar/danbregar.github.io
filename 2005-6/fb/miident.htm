Identifying Characteristics Activity

 

Divide into groups of 4 to 6, for a total of six groups.

 

Each group will be responsible for finding several identifying characteristics about the order that your group is responsible for.  For example, find several identifying characteristics for Order Trichoptera (caddisflies).

 

Use the sections of your document titled larval diagnosis and similar order as references for finding the identifying characteristics.  For our purposes, the identifying characteristics are ways to determine the different orders of aquatic invertebrates from each other.

 

After you have determined several identifying characteristics, pick the best two and write them on the whiteboard under the heading for your order.

 

You have approximately 15 minutes for this activity.

 

Discussion:

 

As a class, we will discuss identifying characteristics that distinguish each order from one another.  Once we have agreed on the identifying characteristics, make sure you copy all of the characteristics for ALL of the orders into your notes.
