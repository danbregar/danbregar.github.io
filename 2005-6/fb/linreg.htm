Age

Height (in inches)

Birth month (as a number)

# of letters in his/her first name

 

2.   Enter your data into a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet.  Put students' names in the left-most column; data fields should run from left to right.

5.   Add a trend line to your graph by right-clicking on one of the data points, clicking on the “Add trendline” option,  clicking on the “Options” tab, selecting “Display R-squared value on chart”, and pressing “OK”.

 

6.   What does your graph show?  According to the R squared value, is there a relationship between the two factors you measured?  Is it a meaningful relationship?
