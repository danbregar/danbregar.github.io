Step 2: Calculate the average values for each data table – type “=average(range)” in a blank cell to find the average.

 

Step 3: Use the T-test function to test the statistical significance of your data – type “=ttest(range1, range2, 2,3) in a blank cell

 

Step 4: Evaluate the results of your T-test – if it is 0.05 or less, you can say “Based on the results of my t-test, there appears to be a difference between the average values of _____ and ____”.  If it is more than 0.05, you can only say “Based on the results of my t-test, there is not enough data to determine whether the average value of ______ is different from the average value of ______”.
