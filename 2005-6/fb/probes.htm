To Fix “Calibration File Not Found” Error:

1.         Go to the File menu and choose Preferences

2.         Click on the Folder Locations Tab

3.         Next to Calibration Folder, click “Browse…”

4.         Select this folder:  “C:\Program Files\Vernier Software\Logger Pro (2.2)\Calibrations”

5.         Click “OK”

6.         Next to Experiments Folder, click “Browse…”

7.         Select this folder:  “C:\Program Files\Vernier Software\Logger Pro (2.2)\Experiments”

8.         Click “OK” (twice) – the calibration procedure should now work correctly

 

To Set Up pH Probe:

1.         Unscrew O-ring cap

2.         Remove pH probe

3.         Remove cap from probe

4.         Rinse with distilled water

 

To Store pH Probe:

1.         Rinse with distilled water

2.         Slide cap back onto probe

3.         Screw bottle back onto cap

 

To Set Up Dissolved Oxygen Probe:

1.                  Unscrew probe cap

2.                  Using the eyedropper, fill the cap with electrode filling solution

3.                  Screw the cap back onto the probe

4.                  Rinse with distilled water

 

To Calibrate Dissolved Oxygen Probe:

1.                  Set up labpro with DO probe

2.                  Launch Logger Pro program

3.                  Go to FILE menu, click OPEN, select “Water Quality with Computers” and “Test 05 Dissolved Oxygen”

4.                  Go to EXPERIMENT menu, click CALIBRATE (if you get an error, look at the top of this document)

5.                  Click “PERFORM NOW”

6.                  Enter “0” for value 1

7.                  Put the DO probe into the 0 mg/L (brown) bottle, wait for it to stabilize – then click “KEEP”

8.                  Rinse with distilled water

9.                  Enter “7.83” for value 2

10.              Fill O-ring bottle ½ full with distilled water

11.              Put probe into O-ring bottle, but make sure probe tip is NOT in the water

12.              Wait for it to stabilize – then click “KEEP”

 

To Store the Dissolved Oxygen Probe:

1.                  Remove the probe cap and gently dump out the electrode filling solution

2.                  Rinse the probe tip and cap with distilled water

3.                  Blot the probe tip and cap dry with a Kimwipe

4.                  Loosely screw the cap back onto the probe and wrap the cable

 

To Calibrate Turbidity Sensor:

1.                  Set up labpro with Turbity sensor

2.                  Launch Logger Pro program

3.                  Go to EXPERIMENT menu, click CALIBRATE (if you get an error, go to the top of this document)

4.                  Click “PERFORM NOW”

5.                  Fill the clear bottle with distilled water

6.                  Wipe the bottle and the inside of the sensor with a Kimwipe and put the bottle in the sensor

7.                  Enter “0” for value 1 – click “KEEP”

8.                  Wipe the cloudy bottle and the inside of the sensor with a Kimwipe and put the bottle in the sensor

9.                  Enter “100” for value 2- click “KEEP”

 

To Set Up Temperature Probe for Remote Operation:

1.                  Set up labpro with Temperature probe

2.                  Put batteries in labpro

3.                  Launch Logger Pro program

4.                  Go to SETUP menu, click “DATA COLLECTION”, choose “SELECTED EVENTS”, and press “OK”

5.                  Go to REMOTE menu, click “SET UP LABPRO”, and press “SETUP”

6.                  Close the Logger Pro program

7.                  Unplug the labpro from the computer

8.                  To take a temperature measurement, wait 30 seconds, then press “Start/Stop”

9.                  To retrieve your data, plug the labpro back into the computer and launch the Logger Pro program

10.              Click on “RETRIEVE DATA”

11.              If you run into problems, remove the batteries from the labpro, unplug it from the computer, close the Logger Pro program, and start over
