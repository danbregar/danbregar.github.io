Online Soil Survey of Benton County

Field Biology

 

Website:  www.or.nrcs.usda.gov/pnw_soil/or_data.html

 

The website above will take you to a menu that will include online soil survey maps of Oregon.  Click on “Maps” on the Benton (OR 003 Benton County) line.  A crude map of Benton County will appear on the screen.  Position the pointer just above Corvallis on the edge of the yellow and click.  You will be able to see a smaller area in more detail.  Click again.  When an aerial photo appears with numbers on it, click on 14 – (the 14 stands for Section 14 in the government’s way of dividing up large areas of land).   Crescent Valley H.S. is in the middle of section 14.  As you focus on the grounds around the school, you will see what appear to be topographic lines – these are actually the boundaries of different soil types in the CV area.  To find out more information about the soil, make sure the “Soils” button is selected as Active on the right hand side of the screen, click on the little “i” on the left hand side of the screen, and click on the soil you are interested.
