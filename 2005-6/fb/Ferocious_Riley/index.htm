Internet Links

URL

Description

http://www.rileythedog.com/eating.htm

This site is a great overall description of Riley.  It explains her most common food sources, and talks about why she is so vicious

http://www.picturesofriley.com

This site is a warehouse of all the pictures of Riley that have been published on line or in print.  It contains over 2000 pictures and new ones are being added each day!

http://www.imasurvivor.com

This site is the true account of someone who survived an encounter with Riley in the wild.  This person (a student at Crescent Valley High School) was nipped over 7 times in the heels and ankles by Riley while she was trying to herd him.  Luckily, he was not injured, and lived to tell the tale.  This site also discussed some of the most common myths about Riley.
