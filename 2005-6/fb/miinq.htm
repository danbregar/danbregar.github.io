Macroinvertebrate Inquiry Project

Field Biology

 

In this project, you will design and carry out a study that involves aquatic macroinvertebrates.  As a class, you will generate a “what is the relationship between” question; you will decide on the best way to collect data to answer the question; and in groups of two, you will collect and interpret your data.  You will summarize the information you have found in an individual written report that includes data tables and graphs.

 

Your timetable will be as follows:

Friday, Nov. 4th:  Introduction to the project; brainstorming ideas

Monday, Nov. 7th:  Brainstorming possible questions and “what is the relationship between” questions

Wednesday, Nov. 16th:  Determine your data collection procedure

Friday, November 18th:  Collect data for your project and begin write-up

Monday, November 21st:  Work on write-up

Wednesday, November 23rd:  Final write-up due
