Agenda for Friday, 10/21/05

Field Biology – Period 7

 

You will each have 15 minutes to take the plant quiz.  During the rest of the 30 minute period, you should do the following:

 

1.   Sign up on the board for one of the four weed groups (trees, shrubs, forbs, grasses) for the Timberhill Project.  In addition to your specific native plant, you will also be looking at some weeds in the group for which you signed up.

2.   With a partner, use one of the laptops to find the following information on the internet.  On a sheet of scratch paper, write down the information that you find along with the URL for each site you get information from!

a)   What are the scientific and common names of at least three plants that are in your weed group?

b)   How can you identify each of these species?

c)   Where did each of these three plants come from originally?  How did they end up in the Willamette Valley (if known)?

d)   What are some of the specific problems associated with each of these weed species?

e)   Are there any successful techniques that have been used to eradicate these weed species?

3.   Make sure your name and your partner’s name are on your sheet of paper and turn it in to the gobble gobble turkey bin.
