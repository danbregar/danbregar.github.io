Intro to GIS Activity

Field Biology

 

 

1.   Go to the Benton County GIS website:  http://gis.co.benton.or.us/v09_10/Source/Container.htm

 

2.   Add aerial photo information from the Legend menu (use Benton County 1 foot images)

 

3.   Locate your house

 

4.   Get the tax lot information to see who is the official owner of your house

 

5.   Select the Soil information in the Identify menu

 

6.   Identify the type of soil in the vicinity of your house

 

7.   Locate your class project site (Chip Ross Park, Timberhill, or the Noyes property)

 

8.   Identify the type of soils in the vicinity of your project site – click in a number of places to see the different types of soils

 

9.   Select the Streams information in the Identify menu

 

10. Locate any wetlands or streams in or near your project site

 

11. Select the Tax Lot information in the Identify menu

 

12. Identify the owner of your project site
