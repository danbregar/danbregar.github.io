Course Description:

This class is a introductory course in programming and Visual Basic.

 

Grading:

Grades will be given at the end of the quarter; a progress grade will be given halfway through the quarter.  Grades will be calculated as a straight percentage of your work; letter grades are determined by a standard 90/80/70/60 scale.  Daily work will constitute 50% of your grade (5 points per day); projects will make up the remaining 50% of your grade.

 

Attendance:

You will succeed in this class if you attend regularly and work hard in class.  I expect you to be on time every day. Students who are chronically late or absent will be placed on an attendance contract that will be strictly enforced.  Five or more unexcused absences in a quarter will result in credit denial (see Student Handbook).

 

Late Work:

If you miss class due to an excused absence, you have one day to make up any assignments you have missed without losing points.  Work turned in 24 hours late will be worth 70% of the total points; work turned in more than 24 hours late will receive a zero.  If you miss class due to an unexcused absence, you are still required to make up assignments you have missed; however, you will receive a zero for your work.  No late work will be accepted during the last week of the quarter.  Students who receive an incomplete grade must submit their missing work within 90 days of the end of the quarter.

 

Plagiarism:

The science department has a zero-tolerance policy for plagiarism.  Plagiarized work will receive a zero, parents will be notified, and a note will be placed in the student’s file.  Students who plagiarize more than once will receive an F for the quarter.  Please note that the science department does not distinguish between plagiarism and cheating.

 

Classroom Procedures

Please follow these rules to make our class run as smoothly as possible!

1.   Please do not bring food or drinks into the computer lab (except bottled water).

2.   Please use the computers only for assigned work.

3.   No hacking!!!

4.   Save all programs and files to floppy disks AND the H: drive.

5.   Please shut down the computers properly at the end of each block.
