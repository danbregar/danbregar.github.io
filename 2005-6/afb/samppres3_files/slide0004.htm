Frazier Creek:
•This site is within the Jackson-Frazier wetlands.
•Mint plants grow abundantly alongside algae and fungus in the damp environment.
•The creek floor is sandy, with no rocks.
•The sluggish creek is around ten feet wide, but with virtually no bank; the water is dissuaded from flooding only by the presence of various thick, wetland grasses.
•This creek retains many of the properties of the surrounding wetlands.
