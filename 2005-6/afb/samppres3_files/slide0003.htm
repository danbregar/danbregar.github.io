Site Locations:
Jackson Creek:
•Located on the Crescent Valley campus, directly South of the gym on the East side of the walkway leading up to it.
•Creek at this location characterized by ripples, steep banks and an abundance of large rocks and loose gravel.
•This site is close to the main part of campus, and may be disturbed by students.
•Plants present are; iris, cow parsnip, English ivy, Himalayan blackberry, horsetail, gallium and Oregon Ash .
