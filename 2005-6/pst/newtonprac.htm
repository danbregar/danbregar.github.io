Practice Quiz –Newton’s

Laws and Forces

Physical Science and Technology

 

Please use your own paper to solve these problems.  Use the Five Steps where indicated.  Make sure to write the title of this assignment (“PQ – Newton’s Laws and Forces”) in the top center of your paper and put your first and last name in the upper right-hand corner.

 

1.     Mr. Bregar is picking his nose in a room where there is no gravity or friction.  He gets a good booger and flicks it EXTREMELY slowly – the booger is moving only 0.0000004 m/s.  The booger does not hit any objects or experience any other forces.  How long will it take the booger to stop moving?

 

2.     Mr. Bregar and Sara Lopez are fighting (Sara is winning).  Sara pushes Mr. Bregar’s backpack with her hand.  According to Newton’s 3rd law, what must also happen in this situation?

 

3.     Mr. Baldwin is standing with his hands out in front of him, holding Mr. Bregar in the air.  Mr. Bregar is not moving at all.  Draw a force diagram showing the forces acting on Mr. Bregar.

 

4.     In problem 3 above, is there a net force on Mr. Bregar?  How do you know?

 

5.     Mr. Bregar is being dragged on the ground by a wild hippo.  Mr. Bregar has a mass of 85 kg and because of the hippo’s pull, he is experiencing an unbalanced force of 154 Newtons.  How quickly is Mr. Bregar accelerating?  Use the Five Steps.

 

6.     Mr. Bregar is being thrown into the air by a disturbed lemming.  Mr. Bregar has a mass of 85 kg and is accelerating at 75 m/s/s.  What is the size of the unbalanced force on Mr. Bregar?  Use the Five Steps.

 

7.     Mr. Bregar is being pushed through a brick wall by an unsavory wildebeest.  The unbalanced force on Mr. Bregar is 98 Newtons, and Mr. Bregar is accelerating at 1.18 m/s/s.  What is Mr. Bregar’s mass?  Use the Five Steps.

 

8.     A gigantic monster is chewing on a bug.  Draw a force diagram for a tooth in the monster’s lower jaw.

 

Extra Credit:  Devon Templeman is pulling Mr. Bregar, who is crying, around in his little red wagon.  No matter how hard she pulls on the little red wagon, it pulls back on her with the exact same force (Newton’s 3rd law).  Explain using WORDS and a DIAGRAM how Devon was able to get the little red wagon started moving at all!
