Acceleration Problems I

Physical Science and Technology

 

Instructions:  Please complete these problems on your own sheet of paper – write your name, today’s date, and the due date (today) in the top left corner of your paper.  Write the title of this assignment (“Acceleration Problems 1”) across the top of your page.  Use the Five Steps where indicated.

 

1.   Mrs. Stroud is running down the street at 7.5 meters per second.  She sees a double-tall latte on the road ahead of her and starts sprinting.  After 3 seconds have passed, Mrs. Stroud is running at 9.1 meters per second.  Assuming she does not change direction, what was Mrs. Stroud’s acceleration?  Use the Five Steps.

 

 

 

2.   Lacey Cochran’s older brother Brandon is falling off a cliff.  At the beginning of his fall, his velocity is 0 meters per second.  After 10 seconds, his velocity is 283 meters per second.  What was Brandon’s acceleration?  (Brandon was uninjured when he hit the ground because he landed on Mr. Bregar).  Use the Five Steps.

 

 

 

3.   Shaina Manley is riding her bike across a parking lot.  She is heading east at 19.2 meters per second.  She runs over Mr. Bregar, who is lying down in the parking lot.  Five seconds after running over Mr. Bregar, Shaina is heading east at 14.8 meters per second.  What was Shaina’s acceleration?  Use the Five Steps.

 

 

4.   Mr. Baldwin throws a pumpkin onto Mr. Bregar’s car.  At first, Mr. Baldwin is holding the pumpkin motionless.  1.3 seconds after Mr. Baldwin lets go, the pumpkin is traveling at 329.8 meters per second.  Assuming it does not change direction, what was the acceleration of the pumpkin?  Use the Five Steps.

 

 

5.   A go-cart is rolling around a track at 6.5 miles per hour.  The track is circular.  The go-cart’s speed does not change.  Is the go-cart accelerating?  WHY???????????  P.S.  Mr. Bregar is being dragged behind the cart.  You do not need to use the Five Steps.
