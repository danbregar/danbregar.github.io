For two separate, moving objects on your Rube Goldberg Machine, measure displacement (the distance between where they start and stop moving), time (how many seconds they move for) , and mass (in kilograms, using a 3-beam balance).  Then use that information to calculate Final Velocity (d/t), Acceleration ((Vf-Vi) / t), and Force (m * a).  Use the blank data sheets below as a template to record your data.
