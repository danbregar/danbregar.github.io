Momentum Problems 3

Physical Science and Technology

 

Please use your own paper to solve these problems.

 

1.   Nicole Buck rolls a bowling ball down the hall towards a frozen turkey that is sitting still in the middle of the floor.  The bowling ball has a mass of 8.2 kg and is moving with a velocity of 5.2 m/s, while the turkey has a mass of 1.4 kg.  The bowling ball slams into the frozen turkey and comes to a complete stop, while the turkey goes flying off. 

a.   What is the bowling ball’s momentum before the collision?  (Use the five steps)

b.   What is the turkey’s momentum after the collision?  (The five steps are not necessary)

c.   What is the velocity of the turkey after the collision?  (Use the five steps)

 

2.   A frozen turkey rolls Mr. Bregar down the hall towards Nicole Buck, who is sitting still in the middle of the floor.  Mr. Bregar has a mass of 74 kg, and Nicole also has a mass of 74 kg.  Mr. Bregar is rolling at a velocity of 0.25 m/s when he collides with Nicole.  As a result of the collision, Mr. Bregar comes to a complete stop and Nicole goes flying.

a.   What is Mr. Bregar’s momentum before the collision?  (Use the five steps)

b.   What is Nicole’s momentum after the collision?  (The five steps are not necessary)

c.   What is Nicole’s velocity after the collision?  (Use the five steps)

 

3.   Mr. Bregar is soaring through the air like a giant bald bird.  Suddenly, he hits a 5,400 kg helicopter which was hovering motionless in the air.  As a result of the collision, Mr. Bregar comes to a complete stop and the helicopter goes flying off.  Mr. Bregar has a mass of 76 kg and was traveling at 1.3 m/s.

a.   What is Mr. Bregar’s momentum before the collision?  (Use the five steps)

b.   What is the helicopter’s momentum after the collision?  (The five steps are not necessary)

c.   What is the velocity of the helicopter after the collision?  (Use the five steps)

 

4.   Mr. Bregar is running straight towards a giant bald turkey that is sitting still in the middle of the road.  Mr. Bregar has a mass of 76 kg and is running at 1.2 m/s.  The turkey has a mass of 64 kg.  As a result of the collision, Mr. Bregar comes to a complete stop and the giant bald turkey goes flying off.

a.   What is Mr. Bregar’s momentum before the collision?  (Use the five steps)

b.   What is the giant bald turkey’s momentum after the collision?  (The five steps are not necessary)

c.   What is the velocity of the giant bald turkey after the collision?  (Use the five steps)
