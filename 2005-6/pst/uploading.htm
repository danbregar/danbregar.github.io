Tips for Uploading Files

Physical Science and Technology

 

#1 most important tip to remember:  Make sure your file names are short (less than 12 characters), and contain no spaces, capital letters, or punctuation!

 

#2 most important tip to remember:  Make sure to save all your files to your H: drive!  Edit and save your homepage to your H: drive before you upload it to Geocities.

 

Here are the general steps for adding a link to your home page and uploading files.

 

1.   Save the new file (the one you’re linking to) to your H: drive.  Remember to use a short file name with no spaces, capital letters, or punctuation.

 

2.   Open the saved version of your home page from your H: drive with Microsoft Frontpage.

 

3.   Create the new link on your home page.  When you enter the name of the link, type the name of the new file you just saved.

 

4.   Log into your Geocities account using Internet Explorer.  Select “Upload Manager”.

 

5.   Click on the first “Browse” button and locate your home page file on your H: drive.

 

6.   Click on the second “Browse” button and locate the new file you saved on your H: drive.

 

7.   Click on “Upload Files”.

 

8.   Test your web page – make sure that we can access it from the list of student web pages (http://www2.corvallis.k12.or.us/cvhs/science/PST0405webpages.htm).
