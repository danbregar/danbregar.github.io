e.        This web site describes how many everyday experiences are related to waves.  Sound, light, and energy are all types of waves.  Sound is caused by waves of pressure in the air – pressure waves that vibrate quickly make high-pitched sounds, and waves that are very strong make loud sounds.  Since sound is caused by air molecules vibrating and pushing on each other, sound cannot be transmitted without air (so in outer space, there is no sound).  Sound can be transmitted through wood, plastic, and water, but it doesn’t sound the same as it does through air.
