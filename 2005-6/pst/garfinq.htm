Inquiry Project for Garfield School

Physical Science and Technology

 

Purpose:  To create an interactive, technology-based activity that is portable so we can take it to Garfield Elementary School and have Garfield students learn the physical science associated with the technology.

 

Your specific goal will be to create a laboratory-type activity that will allow someone to manipulate or adjust a device and take measurements in order to find out how the adjustment affected the device’s operation.  An example would be an adjustable ramp with a motion sensor attached to it – someone could adjust the height of the ramp and then measure the velocity of an object rolling down the ramp.  This project would allow someone to answer the question “How does the height (slope) of a ramp relate to the velocity of an object moving down the ramp?”.  Your project should allow someone to answer a similar question.

 

Step 1:  Develop a question that your device will be designed to answer.  Use the probes, sensors, and measuring tools listed below to help you decide what to measure.  Have your question approved by an instructor.  Target date:  Friday, 1/7/05

 

Step 2:  Make 10 thumbnail sketches for your device.  Target date:  Friday, 1/7/05

 

Step 3:  Find two other people in the class who are interested in a device similar to yours.  Have your group members approved by an instructor.  Target date:  Monday, 1/10/05

 

Step 4:  Pick one device for your group to build, test, and demonstrate.  Make a quick full-page sketch of your final idea and have it approved by an instructor.  Target date:  Monday, 1/10/05

 

Step 5:  Build and test your device.  Target date:  Thursday, 1/13/05

 

Step 6:  Make a web page that will provide instructions for using the device, guidelines for using the measuring tools, and questions that will help other students conduct an investigation of their own design.  Target date:  Thursday, 1/13/05

 

Step 6:  Demonstrate your device and let other students test it.  Due date:  Tuesday, 1/18/05

 

Students whose projects work reliably will be visiting Garfield Elementary between Wednesday, 1/19/05 and Friday, 1/21/05.
