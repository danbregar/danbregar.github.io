Physical Science and Technology

 

Project Introduction:

Science is much more than a body of knowledge, of mere facts.  It is a way of understanding our world and, equally important, it is a process following certain criteria.  The scientific endeavor utilizes our observations of the natural world to construct models describing how the world seems to operate. 

 

There are a number of things that make the scientific endeavor unique from other endeavors.  All scientific research builds on existing knowledge.  When a problem is identified, researchers spend extensive time researching the problem, attempting to answer such questions as

What is known about the topic?

What has past research on your topic discovered?

How have previous investigations been carried out?

What have previous researchers suggested for future investigations?

 

Today’s assignment is intended to assist you in selecting a topic for your research project in this class.  This assignment will also help you get started with the research process -- the initial step to all scientific research.
