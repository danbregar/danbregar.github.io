It's Graduation Time!

Seniors - Have you paid your NHS dues for 2004-2005?  If so, please visit Mr. Bregar in room F-18 during the week of June 6 - 10 to pick up your honor cord.  Stop by during lunch, before or after school, or during B block.

 

Spring clean-up is Sunday, June 12, from 3-5 pm!  All sophomores and juniors in NHS must attend.  Please bring yard tools, gloves, and wheelbarrows to help clean campus up for graduation!

 

PAY YOUR DUES!!!!

 

$5 due by Friday, December 17 for all members.

 

Members initiated last spring who have NOT paid their dues for last year owe an additional $5 as a late fee.

 

After December 17, there will be an additional $20 late fee.

 

If you have not paid your dues by Friday, May 27, you will no longer be in NHS!  Pay early and avoid the hassle ...

 

Please pay Mr. Bregar in F-18 with check or cash.

 

VOLUNTEER!!!!

 

You need 10 hours of community service per semester - check out the volunteer opportunities or talk to Mr. Bregar to find out what your options are.
