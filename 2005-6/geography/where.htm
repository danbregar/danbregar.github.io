Discussion Questions:

1. Why do you think that there are so many people from so many different countries on the Resolution?  Why don’t we just pick people from the United States?

 

2. What kinds of problems might come about due to the different languages that people speak on the ship?  How do you think that those problems are solved?

 

3. How do you think that all the different workers get to the Resolution from their homes?  Who do you think organizes this and how do they do it?

 

4. Some of the people in the videos spoke different languages.  If you wanted to find out what language they were speaking, what would you do?  What if you wanted to translate what they said – how could you do that?
