Meeting Agenda - 12/14/05

 

National Honor Society is a national organization that includes members who exemplify service, character, and academic success.  Membership in NHS is by invitation; each spring, a new set of prospective members will be invited to join NHS.

 

The minimum GPA requirement for NHS is 3.5 to join; a 3.33 is required to sustain membership.

 

In order to remain in NHS and receive an honor cord at graduation, all members are expected to fulfill the following requirements:

 

Please note that NHS membership obligations have changed for the 2005-2006 school year.  Please click here for a summary of the changes and the reasons behind these change.

 

1.  Participate in a student-generated community service project that involves at least 15 hours of work (hours will be prorated for the class of 2006)

 

2.  Participate in at least three NHS volunteer opportunities such as the campus clean-up, Initiation, or Fall Festival

 

3.  Pay $10 in dues during May of their senior year (this will be prorated to $5 for the class of 2006)

 

4.  Attend mandatory NHS meetings that will occur roughly once each month

 

5.  Sign the NHS consent form indicating student and parent understanding of these NHS obligations
