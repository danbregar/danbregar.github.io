What Trying Looks Like

Mr. Bregar’s Classes

 

When you try your best to succeed in one of my classes, here is what I will see:

1.   You show up for class every day and on time.

2.   You make good work of class time when it is available to work on assignments.

3.   You participate in class discussions either by listening carefully or by contributing relevant questions or comments.

4.   Every assignment is turned in on time and done to the best of your ability.

5.   When you have questions about course material or assignments, you discuss these questions with Mr. Bregar.

6.   You contribute to a positive classroom environment and are not disruptive to other students.

7.   You take advantage of any extra credit or optional activities to increase your understanding.

8.   You are respectful of everyone in the class (including yourself!).

9.   You listen and take notes when appropriate.

10. You are prepared to learn and work every day.
