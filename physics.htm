Physics

 

Physics is a standard high school class that covers non-calculus-based physics. We will study motion and forces in one and two directions, rotational motion and torques, electricity and magnetism, and possibly additional topics if time allows. Our class Showbie codes are MFHN5 (2nd period), CFABP (3rd period) and M6EVP (6th period).
