Physics

 

Physics is a standard high school class that covers non-calculus-based physics. We will study motion and forces in one and two directions, rotational motion and torques, electricity and magnetism, and possibly additional topics if time allows. Our class Showbie codes are QDRQ2 (5th period), and MTTLN (7th period).
