Genetic Diseases

Field Biology

 

Genetic variation is a key factor in evolution.  Genetic variation can lead to beneficial, positive traits that improve an organism’s biological fitness – or it can lead to harmful conditions, often referred to as genetic diseases.

 

In humans, we have identified dozens of genetic diseases.  These diseases can be mild or they can be debilitating.  The following web site details many of the genetic diseases that humans can have.
